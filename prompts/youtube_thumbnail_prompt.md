# YouTube Thumbnail Prompt

Generate a cinematic editorial thumbnail for WetSea Observatory.

## Objectives
- Communicate technical exploration of maritime/global systems.
- Remain legible at small sizes.
- Maintain premium restraint.

## Layout
- One dominant subject.
- One short headline (3–6 words).
- One technical sublabel.
- Clean margins, no clutter.

## Forbidden
- Clickbait arrows/faces.
- Neon typography.
- Excessive contrast pumping.
