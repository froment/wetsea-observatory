# WetSea Observatory — Knowledge Base

## 1. Brand Essence
WetSea Observatory is an editorial research identity exploring oceanic and infrastructural realities: submarine cables, ports, logistics corridors, maritime telemetry, and cloud-era physical systems.

### Core Attributes
- Calm over spectacle.
- Precision over noise.
- Observation over promotion.
- Timelessness over trend.

## 2. Narrative Territories
- Ocean intelligence and bathymetric systems.
- Invisible infrastructures and cable landing points.
- Ports as geopolitical interfaces.
- Signal routes between seabed and cloud architecture.
- Technical field documentation.

## 3. Tone of Voice
- Analytical, literate, restrained.
- Never hype-driven.
- Documentary and systems-oriented.
- Confident but understated.

## 4. Persistent Visual DNA
- Strong negative space.
- Cinematic framing.
- Layered information hierarchy.
- Instrument-inspired overlays (grids, labels, coordinates).
- Aged-modern tactility (grain, print discipline, low saturation).

## 5. Anti-Patterns
Avoid all outputs resembling:
- cyberpunk,
- gaming UI,
- startup SaaS dashboards,
- neon futurism,
- crypto iconography,
- generic AI art.
